ABOUT MESCOM

MESCOM is a leading power distribution company in Karnataka, India.

Established in June 2002, it serves over 8 million consumers across 8 districts in the state, committed to providing reliable and affordable electricity. Its headquarters are located in Mangalore, Karnataka.

MISSION

"To empower lives through excellence in power distribution."

Meeting the growing demand for electricity in their region. Improving the quality and reliability of power supply. Promoting energy efficiency and conservation. Providing excellent customer service.
